# Awesome Gift Prompts

A collection of prompts to use on [ProfileGift][1] in order to find the perfect
gifts.

Sharing suggestions and contributions about great prompts that worked for you
are welcome!

## Handyman husband ([try it][handyman-husband])

> I’m looking for a gift for my husband who loves DIY electronics. He spends his
> weekends 3D printing parts for his vintage video game consoles. He loves
> open-source software and hates tools that break easily. Budget: $120.

## Grandmother cook ([try it][grandmother-cook])

> I need a gift for my 75-year-old grandmother. She is an expert baker who makes
> complex French pastries. She has a large garden and mild arthritis in her
> hands. She hates clutter and prefers high-quality, durable tools. Budget: $100.

## Yoga sister ([try it][yoga-sister])

> I need a gift for my sister who practices yoga daily. She is currently
> stressed at work and is trying to create a calming morning routine. She
> loves natural textures and earthy colors. She dislikes strong perfumes.
> Budget: $60.


## Plant mom ([try it][plant-mom])

> I’m looking for a gift for a busy mother of two who loves plants but forgets
> to water them. She has a dog (so plants must be pet-safe) and her apartment
> doesn’t get much direct sunlight. She loves natural materials. Budget: $80.


## Retired father-in-law ([try it][retired-father-in-low])

> I need a gift for my 65-year-old father-in-law. He is retired and spends his
> evenings listening to vintage jazz vinyl on a tube amplifier. He loves sipping
> very dark, strong craft beers. He values craftsmanship and hates cheap
> plastic gadgets. Budget: $150.


## Minimalist developer ([try it][minimalist-developer])

> I’m looking for a gift for a 30-year-old Full Stack Developer. He loves
> minimalist design, hates loud noises, and drinks espresso. Budget: $100.


## Sporty wife ([try it][sporty-wife])

> My wife is in her thirties and appreciates an healthy lifestyle. She loves
> good tea, and practices running and voleyball several times a week. I have
> a budget of $90.


## Geeky teenager ([try it][geeky-teenager])

> My 15 years old son plays to video games like Fortnite and Minecraft. He often
> spends hours playing online with friends. He also enjoys practicing biking with
> his friends. Budget: $80.
